Most Asked Questions

1. What does e-rickshaw insurance cover?

E-rickshaw insurance typically covers damages caused by accidents, theft, fire, natural disasters, and third-party liabilities arising from bodily injury or property damage.

2. How do I purchase e-rickshaw insurance?

You can purchase e-rickshaw insurance by contacting our customer service representatives. Simply provide your vehicle details and select the coverage options that best suit your needs.

3. What are the requirements for obtaining e-rickshaw insurance?

The requirements may vary, but generally, you need to provide vehicle registration documents, proof of ownership, and identification. Additionally, compliance with local regulations and safety standards may be necessary.

4. What types of accidents or damages are covered under the policy?

E-rickshaw insurance covers various accidents and damages, including collisions, vandalism, theft, fire, floods, and third-party liabilities resulting from accidents.

5. How do I file a claim for e-rickshaw insurance?

To file a claim, you need to notify us immediately after an incident and provide relevant details such as the incident report, photos of the damages, and any other supporting documents. Our claims team will guide you through the process.

6. Why should I have Insurance?

Insurance provides financial protection and peace of mind by covering unexpected events or emergencies, helping mitigate risks and reducing the financial burden of potential losses.
